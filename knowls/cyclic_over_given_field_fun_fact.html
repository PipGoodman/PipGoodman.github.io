Let $A/\mathbb{Q}$ be an abelian surface such that $\mathrm{Gal}(\mathbb{Q}(A[2])/\mathbb{Q}) \cong C_5$. Then $\mathrm{End}(A_{\overline{\mathbb{Q}}}) \otimes \mathbb{Q}$ is isomorphic to either $\mathbb{Q}$ or $\mathbb{Q}(\sqrt{5})$. Moreover, all endomorphisms of $A$ are defined over $\mathbb{Q}$.
