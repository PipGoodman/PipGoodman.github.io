Pip Goodman

Postdoctoral researcher working with Galois representations and endomorphism algebras of abelian varieties

Hello and welcome to my website! I'm a postdoc researcher in number theory at the Max Planck Institute in Bonn. Previously I held an early career fellowship with the LMS. I completed my PhD at the University of Bristol under the supervision of Tim Dokchitser.

My work focuses on the interactions between Galois representations and endomorphism algebras attached to abelian varieties. For example, what constraints do the images of the residual representations impose on the endomorphism ring? Or what Galois groups can be realised via Galois representations attached to abelian varieties with non-trivial endomorphism ring?
